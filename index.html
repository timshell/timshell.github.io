overview

I am currently building Roundtable (YC S23) with Matt Hardy. Roundtable builds artificial intelligence infrastructure for user and market researchers.

Previously, I was a PhD Student in Psychology and Neuroscience at Princeton University, advised by Jon Cohen, Tom Griffiths, and Nathaniel Daw. Before that, I studied Computer Science and Philosophy at Swarthmore College. My research overview can be accessed here.

My interests have led me to stints elsewhere in the academic (Oxford, Carnegie Mellon, Penn), commercial (Kallyope, Mercury Fund), and nonprofit (Breakthrough Collaborative) sectors. You can see a blog post of my previous work here.
